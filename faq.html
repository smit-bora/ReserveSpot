Reserve Spot - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Get answers to all your questions about our parking reservation service.

1. How does Reserve Spot work?
Reserve Spot is a simple and convenient platform where users can reserve parking spots in advance. You can select your spot, pick the date and time, and book your parking hassle-free. Our platform provides real-time availability, so you always know which spots are open.
2. How much does it cost to reserve a parking spot?
Our pricing is simple and affordable:
  • 1-4 hours: ₹20
  • 4-12 hours: ₹50
  • 12-24 hours (full day): ₹100
No matter how long you stay, the maximum you’ll pay in a day is ₹100.
3. Can I reserve a parking spot for more than 24 hours?
Currently, we only offer reservations for up to 24 hours at a time. You can make a new reservation for additional days if needed, but each reservation is limited to a 24-hour period.
4. Is it safe to park at the reserved spots?
Yes! All reserved parking spots are in secure areas monitored around the clock to ensure the safety of your vehicle.
5. Can I cancel or modify my reservation?
You can cancel or modify your reservation up to one hour before the reserved time without any penalty. After that, cancellations may not be refunded, so make sure to plan ahead.
6. How do I make payments for my parking reservation?
Payments are made through our secure, contactless payment system. We accept all major payment methods, including UPI, credit cards, and digital wallets.
7. What happens if the parking spot I reserved is taken when I arrive?
If this happens, contact our support team immediately. We will ensure you get an alternative spot or assist you with a refund. Our goal is to ensure a smooth parking experience for all users.
8. How can I check the availability of parking spots?
Reserve Spot provides real-time availability for all parking spots on the platform. You can view available spots on the map during the booking process.
